Environmental baseline studies

Environmental baseline studies in air quality are required by czech air protection law 201/2012 for every company who need to obtain a binding opinion and permission to operate factories. You will need it:

  • if you are planning to place new stationary source into indutrial zone
  • or after every alteration of stationary source in a factory (increase of working hours, exchange of old source for a more powerful one etc.)
  • or if regional authohority requires it as a part of IPPC

Environmental baseline study is done according to annex no. 13 Decree no. 415/2012 Sb.

Request for permission to operate the factory

For all owners of stationary sources covered by Act § 41 paragraph 4, 5 a 6 of the law no. 201/2012 Coll. required by regional authority.

Request is made according to annex no. 7 of law no. 201/2012 Coll.

The request for permission to operate the factory must also include Operational guidelines for factories according to annex no. 2 of law no. 201/2012 Coll.

Operational guidelines for factories

according to annex no. 12 Decree no. 415/2012 Coll.

Integrated pollution, prevention and control (IPPC)

IPPC is required by czech authorities according to the czech law no. 76/2002 Sb.. We will:

  • Process and submit an application for IPPC procedure
  • Discuss the application with all relevant authorities
  • Ensure approval of the changes and updated in IPPC
  • Preparation of annual reports on implementation of the integrated permit conditions

Integrated air pollution register (IRZ)

Applied for all businesses whose technology releases waster, air pollutants to the environment according to the annex no. I of government regulation no. 166/2006/ES.

Limits set for the reporting obligation are:

  • 2 tons of hazardous waste per year
  • 2000 tons of another waste per year

Integrated system for reporting obligations (ISPOP)

We will fill in and submit an annual report ISPOP for you. ISPOP monitors the release of air pollutants. Also we will calculate the amount of ozone-depleting substances under the Act no. 73/2012 Coll. like:

  • Halons and halon instances
  • Controlled substances and fluorinated greenhouse gases (F-gases)

Greenhouse gases accounting

The verification of greenhouse gas emissions concerns two EU law: Commission Regulation no. 601/2012, monitoring and reporting of greenhouse gas emissions, and Commission Regulation no. 600/2012, on verification of greenhouse gas emissions and tonne-kilometer data reports and accreditation of verifiers.
